How WhatsApp checkout works

Complete your order in three quick steps. Need help? Talk to support.

Step 1

Confirm your products

Review the items pulled in from your cart. You can still edit your cart or add more products before sending.

Step 2

Share your delivery details

Fill in the form with your name, phone number, and preferred delivery option. The WhatsApp message is generated automatically — simply hit “Send”.

Step 3

Get order confirmation

Our pharmacist team replies on WhatsApp with your delivery ETA and payment instructions. Live chat hours are 8am–9pm EAT, Monday to Sunday.

Delivery & payment options

We currently fulfil across Uganda. If you are outside Uganda, contact support and we will recommend a partner.

Delivery windows

  • Store pickup (free): Collect from our Ntinda lab or any partner listed on the support page.
  • Kampala delivery (UGX 5,000): Same-day dispatch for orders placed before 3pm. Evening deliveries available on request.
  • Upcountry courier (UGX 12,000): Shipments go out every weekday via secured pharmacy couriers with tracking updates.

Ways to pay

  • Cash on delivery: Available for Kampala, Entebbe, and Wakiso. Please prepare exact change for faster handover.
  • Mobile Money: Pay via MTN or Airtel on +256 750 491 105. Share the confirmation screenshot in the WhatsApp chat.
  • Corporate invoicing: Clinics and spas can request monthly billing through niveraproductsltd@gmail.com.

Need pharmacist guidance?

Share photos or concerns in the WhatsApp chat — a pharmacist will tailor routine suggestions before you pay. You can also submit the routine builder for a detailed plan.

Prefer a call? Reach us on +256 750 491 105 or email niveraproductsltd@gmail.com.
