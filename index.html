Frequently asked questions

Quick answers on shipping, consultations, and routine support.

How do I qualify for free delivery?

Add products worth UGX 120,000 or more and delivery within Kampala is complimentary. The cart tracks your progress against the threshold.

Do you ship outside Uganda?

We deliver nationwide through our partner pharmacies. Regional East African shipping can be arranged by contacting support.

Can I consult a pharmacist before purchase?

Yes. Use the routine builder or book a WhatsApp consult and a pharmacist will review your routine within 48 hours.

What payment options are available?

We accept mobile money, card payments via partner pharmacies, and cash-on-delivery in Kampala. Clinics can request an invoice for bulk orders.
